Dimond bridge and Sky Bicycle (Tbilisi – Dashbashi Canyon)

General Tour Description:

Spend adventure day with us: explore hanging glass bridge, enjoy café with 360 panoramic view, ride a sky bike over the canyon, feel the flavor of Georgian dishes and get involved in optional masterclasses – this is your memorable day towards Dashbashi canyon.

  • Departure from Tbilisi;
  • Visit Dashbashi canyon; Dimond Bridge and Dashbashi canyon;

    Glass Bridge over the 280 m deep canyon and diamond-shape café in the middle of the bridge, river flowing underneath, impressive scenery, Sky bike, Zip line - each and all make this destination one of visited for the travelers, who enjoy nature and looking for something fun, adventurous and extreme to explore.

  • Lunch at Kass Land Diamond Resort (optional);

    Kass Land Diamond Resort is oriented to create a diverse ecotourism space in a protected area. The restaurant of the complex offer Georgian and European dishes and beverages.

  • Departure to Kiketi Farm (optional);

    Located 30 kilometers away from Tbilisi, at the elevation 1100 above the sea level, Kiketi farm is multifunctional, recreational, cultural and educational space. Spread over 10 hectares, there is wine cellar, horse stable, creative and educational space, rose garden, restaurant, two cafes, gallery, yoga house, hotels and two tree houses.

  • Masterclass of rug weaving in Manglisi convent (optional);

    Masterclass in the monastery where nuns can teach ancient tradition of carpet and rug weaving with natural wool materials, featuring Georgian ornaments, geometric designs and deep, calm colors.

  • Departure to Tbilisi.
