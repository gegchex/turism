Batumi - Adventure Tour

General Tour Description:

Located between the Black Sea and Kolkheti Plain, Shekvetili Dendrological Park contains more than 200 trees, some of which are exotic and older than 300 years, 58 species of birds and animals, among them pink flamingos, pelicans, cormorants, and African cranes; And enjoy natural beauty of lakes and rivers at Kolkheti National Park – “Georgian Amazon”, where you can benefit from various types of boat tours like Pontoon; Kayak and Motorboat; explore Georgian cuisine and find your favorite local dishes, at the end of the day, enjoy Tsitsinatela amusement park with 36 entertainment attractions designed for all ages.

  • Departure from Batumi;

    Batumi is the second largest city in Georgia and is considered to be the “Las Vegas of the Black Sea”. The city is located at the Black Sea coast, surrounded by the Caucasian mountains, in a subtropical climate zone, offering big variety of modern hotels, restaurants, casinos, historical sites and active religious centers, night life and lots of activities.

  • Visit Shekvetili Dendrological park;

    Shekvetili Dendrological park located in between of the Black Sea and the Kolkheti plain, with the view of the Caucasian mountains, hosting exotic species of Flora and Fauna from around the world among them pink flamingos, pelicans, cormorants, African cranes; 200 trees, some of which are exotic and older than 300 years. The park is spread at 60 hectares.

  • Coffee break on request;
  • Visit Kolkheti national park

    Kolkheti National Park referred as “Georgian Amazon” is the only national park that is included in the World heritage UNESCO list. Wetlands of the park are an important resting site for thousand birds migration between Northern Eurasia and Africa especially in late spring and early autumn.
    In Kolkheti National Park various types of boat tours are offered: Pontoon; Kayak; Motorboat. All the services in the park should be booked in advance.

  • Dinner at the Fish farm in Kobuleti (on requst);
  • Visit Tsitsinatela park;

    Tsitsinatela Amusement Park offer entertainments for every age. There are 36 amusement attractions in the park: 8 of them are designed for children, 17 – for family and 11 are extreme attractions. Park also offers children's maze - "Happy City", karting and bowling.
    Park is open from 6 P.M. every day.

  • Visit Miniature Park;

    Georgia in Miniature is the park where you can explore 50 Georgian historical or architectural sites in 30 minutes. Taken in small scales but reflecting all details of the sites Miniature park is hosting several important monuments of the country.

  • Return to Batumi.
