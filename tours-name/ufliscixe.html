Tour to Gori and cave city – Uplistsikhe

General Tour Description:
  • Departure to Gori;

    Once an important midlevel stronghold currently Gori is an important city located on the main highway connecting east and west parts of the country. The city is famous for its orchards, endemic grapes, historical and religious sites, restaurants and wineries, art shops. The city is located on the confluence of two rivers: Mtkvari (same as Kura) and Liakhvi.

  • Visit the museum of Stalin;

    Located in the center of Gori, Museum of Stalin is the most visited site in the city. The museum is divided in three parts: main two-floored building; pavilion with the hut where Stalin was born and Green Pullman carriage. Main two-storied building was constructed by Georgian architect Archil Kurdiani in 1957 and hosts about 40 thousand artifacts connected to the life of Stalin. Personality of Stalin is still controversial and topic of discussion between the scholars because of its harsh reforms.

  • Departure to Uplistsikhe;
  • Visit cave city – Uplistsikhe;

    Uplistsikhe is the cave city located at the bank of river Mtkvari (same as Kura). It is the ancient hand made complex carved in the rocks in Georgia. The oldest dwelling dates back to the end of II and beginning of I millennium BC. Several thousand years later, Uplistsikhe still can impress the visitors with its well designed districts, main streets, amphitheater, temples, living quarters, bakeries, royal halls, wine cellars, stone grape presser and even the pharmacy.

  • Lunch in the local restaurant next to Uplistsikhe (on request);
  • Visit Vacheishvili Marani, enjoy lunch and masterclass
    of Georgian dishes (on request);

    Vacheishvili Marani is located in a remarkable historical site of Atheni Gorge and can impress the visitor with old vineyard terraces, gardens, local farm and natural eco products. At Vacheishvili Marani you can enjoy lunch and masterclass of Georgian dishes: making of Georgian sweet – churchkhela, Khinkali (meat dumpling), Shoti – Georgian bread (on request and booking in advance); The owner of the complex produces goat cheese, honey and all products they offer to the guest at lunch. The location of the Vacheishvili Marani is historically known as one of the oldest traditional wine terroirs and the only macrozone where “Atenuri” wine originates from. Atenuri has been praised since the meddle ages and was recognized as “the wine of the kings”.

  • Return Tbilisi.

    The restaurants of Sighnaghi offer delicious and authentic traditional food, equipped with terraces overlooking the Caucasian Mountains and Alazani Valley.
