    Located among the Caucasian mountains, next to the mountainous river Aragvi - medieval complex Ananuri offers picturesque views and scenery. At the site there are orthodox churches with unique facade decorations, tower to explore, mini souvenir market and shops.

  • Visit Martvili Monastery (on request);

    Constructed on the peak of the hill, Martvili Monastery has the view of Kholkheti Valley and Caucasian slopes. Originally, the main church was constructed in VII century on the pre-Christian site. Ancient history and architecture, impressive scenery and calm surrounding evoke some meditative tranquility while visiting the site.

  • Departure to Prometheus cave;
  • Visit Prometheus cave;

    Prometheus cave is a fairy world of stalagmites and stalactites that triggers the mind and your fantasy; Colorfully illuminated underground world of Prometheus cave is possible to walk through the well-arranged path. The length of the tourist path is 1420 m and includes 5 underground halls. The optional offering for the visitors is the boat trip from the caves. Please, be aware: Visit to Prometheus cave and boat trip could be restricted due to the weather conditions.
