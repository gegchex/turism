Mountain adventure in Gudauri

General Tour Description:
  • Departure to from Tbilisi;
  • Photo stop at Zhinvali water reservoir;

    Zhinvali dam was constructed in 1986 as the project of Hydroelectric power station and water supply to the capital. The reservoir is constructed on the river Aragvi, deepest part is 75 m. Almost half of the population in Tbilisi is provided with the tap water from the Zhinvali water reservoir.

  • Visit Ananuri Fortress;

    Located among the Caucasian mountains, next to the mountainous river Aragvi - medieval complex Ananuri offers picturesque views and scenery. At the site there are orthodox churches with unique facade decorations, tower to explore, mini souvenir market and shops.

  • Departure to Gudauri;

    Gudauri is the most popular winter resort in Georgia. There are 15 working cable lines, approximately 76 km of ski runs, modern hotels and apartment, cozy restaurant and bars. If you like extreme winter sports you can enjoy snowboarding, heliskiing, paragliding. Winter season starts in December and ends in April.

  • Enjoy panoramic view from the Monument of Friendship;

    The Monument of friendship was constructed in 1983 as the symbol of Friendship between the two neighbor countries: Georgia and Russia. The wall is decorated with mosaics depicting Georgian and Russian history, mythology and traditions. The impressive part of the site is the scenery of Caucasian mountains, waterfall and beginning of the river Aragvi.

  • Paragliding, Carting, Qooder bike riding (Quadro riding) on request;
  • Fresh halal lamb barbeque at open space in the mountains (on request);

    If booked in advance, halal barbeques could be made by the local shepherds at the mountains.

  • Visit Stephantsminda;
  • Jeep ride to Gergeti Trinity Church;

    Located on the peak of one of the Caucasian mountains Gergeti Holy Trinity Church is the one of the most attractive sites for the visitors as well as locals. Alpine scenery, background of the third highest mountain Mkinvartsveri (same as Kazbegi) and XIV century architecture make the site “a must see” location. Constructed up in the clouds Gergeti monastery once was the safekeeping space for the locals but now the place where you can enjoy peace and tranquility.

  • Enjoy the view of Mkinvartsveri (same as Kazbegi Mountain);

    Mkinvartsveri, also referred as Kazbegi, is the third highest mountain in Georgia. Elevation of the mountain is 5 054 m above the sea level. The top of the mountain is always snowy and often hidden in the heavy clouds. That is the reason why the local people compare Kazbegi mountain to the shy bride concealing the face under the clouds of veil.

  • Return to Tbilisi.
